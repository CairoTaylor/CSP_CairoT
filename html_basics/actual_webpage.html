Minecraft

Why is it fun?

Minecraft is fun becuase of its playability. The playability of a game is how fun it is to keep playing the game over and over again. Minecraft has muiltiple options for gameplay, and within those options there are "endless possibilities," as they put it. The survival mode is like real life. You must survive with what you can find and if you don't you will die, but coming back to life is allowed infinitley. The ultimate goal is to slay a dragon in another dimesion, but you first have to get there.
Another mode to play in is creative, which puts you in an all powerful, god-like state. You have any resource you could ever wish for, in unlimited amounts. You can control the world exactly how you want it, and you can even fly!

About the game

Minecraft was developed by the Swedish Marcus Persson on May 17, 2009. The game was originally called Cave Game, and it was developed in only 6 days! However, it took many months to get any updates for the game out. Currently, there are over 150 creatures, over 800 unique blocks, and over 1,600 items in the game as a whole, not including hundreds of modpacks with extra entities, blocks, items, and sometimes even new, strange, or more realistic physics. Minecraft is currently in developmental stages for version 1.21.6 for Java, and most likely going to keep updating for a while.
