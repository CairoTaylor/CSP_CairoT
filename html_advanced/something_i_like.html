Cuphead

What is the story?

Cuphead and his younger brother Mugman live with their grandpa(?), but one day, they end up gambling their souls to the Devil. In order to save their souls, they must beat up about 25 other soul debtors and give the "soul contracts"they collect to the Devil in order to save their own.

Here is a list of the debtors you must fight from the first of the isles:

The Root Pack
A group of giant vegetables who don't really know how to fight.
Ribby and Croaks
A pair of boxing, bruising frog brothers.
Goopy Le Grande
A slime that knows how to bounce good and punch better.
Hilda Berg
Half human, half blimp, and able to turn into the moon.
Cagney Carnation
A big flower that shoots seeds and dances 'till he dies.

Here is a list of the debtors you must fight from the seccond of the isles:

Baroness Von Bon Bon
Angry candy queen with a possible appetite for children.
Djimmi the Great
A powerful genie that has his own version of both Pinocchio and Bill Cipher.
Beppi the Clown
A clown that lives at a theme park and has shape-shifting powers.
Wally Warbles
A grumpy but strong bird with a scrawny, braniac son.
Grimm Matchstick
A stuttering dragon that never stops shooting fire.

Here is a list of the debtors you must fight from the third of the isles:

Rumor Honeybottoms
The British queen bee of a honey corperation.
Werner Werman
The German rat army general who is stuck in the past.
Captain Brineybeard
The fearless pirate captain with a living boat.
Cala Maria
A giant mermaid with gorgon powers.
Sally Stageplay
An actress with a mean umbrella.
Dr. Kahl's Robot
A giant robot that gets more powerful the more he is damaged.
Phantom Express
A ghoul train full of ghouls that is also driven by a ghoul.

At this point, if the you have gotten all of the contracts, you must fight the Devil's pit boss, the dapper dealer, King Dice. If you manage to beat at least 3 of his 9 minions and then him, you can move on to the Devil himself. You can choose between giving him the contracts or not, which might make him angry...
